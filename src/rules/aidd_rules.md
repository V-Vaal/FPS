## AIDD Rules for FPS

- **AI as assistant**: AI tools propose changes; humans review and decide.
- **Traceable decisions**: Important modeling and architectural decisions must be logged with rationale.
- **Guardrails over guesswork**: Prefer explicit constraints and validation checks to unconstrained AI generation.
- **Small, reviewable changes**: Break AI-generated code into small, testable units.
- **Continuous verification**: Integrate linting, testing, and basic sanity checks into the development loop.
